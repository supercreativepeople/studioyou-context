**HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT**

This map follows the complete creator journey from first idea to audience at scale. For every stage, it identifies the StudioYou module that owns it, the tools and platforms that serve it, the nature of the relationship to StudioYou (how the moon orbits Jupiter), and the current relationship status.

The lens for every entry is the same: how does this fit the StudioYou mission before we ask whether it competes. Single-lane tools are tenants on the lot or rev-share partners. Integrated OS challengers are true competitive threats — and there are none.

**Relationship types are defined as follows:**

| Relationship Type | Definition |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Enterprise Inclusion / Rev Share** | Tool recommended or embedded inside SY. SY earns rev share on subscriptions and upgrades generated through the platform. |
| **Featured App / Integration** | Tool surfaces in the Production module or relevant module. May be co-marketed. Earns rev share. |
| **Education Partner** | Platform contributes curriculum, certifications, or expert content. Strengthens the Future You knowledge base. |
| **Community Organization** | Industry body, association, or advocacy group. Provides creator access, legitimacy, and distribution at scale. |
| **Strategic Partnership** | Material operational overlap — IP, distribution, infrastructure, or investment. Requires NDA and structured conversation. |
| **Competitive — Monitor** | Closest overlap product. Not a partner candidate. Tracked for positioning and differentiation updates. |
