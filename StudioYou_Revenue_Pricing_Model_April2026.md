# **The Pricing Principle**

StudioYou is not a tool bundle. It is an operating condition. What MrBeast and Dhar Mann built over a decade — the system that catches, documents, protects, and amplifies everything they create — StudioYou delivers to the prosumer creator on day one. That is not a feature set. That is a transformation. The price reflects the transformation, not the inputs.

The creator subscription is the smallest revenue stream by design. The ecosystem — tool partners, brands, agencies — pays to be near the creator base SY builds. The subscription is not the business model. It is the filter. It separates the serious from the meek, funds the operating condition, and establishes the creator as someone who has committed to building a real studio.

*Future You is the singular expression of this value. No course teaches you in real time. No manager is present at the moment of decision. Future You is — always, only in your corner, because it IS you. No educational product, no coaching platform, no tool subscription delivers that. It is a different category entirely.*

# **Creator Subscription Tiers**

## **Studio — The Guided Creator**

Future You in directive mode. Curated tool stack managed by the platform. Full formation experience. Every module. No infrastructure decisions required. This is the operating condition available to someone who has never had it — and could not assemble it independently at any price.

| Interval | Price | Effective Monthly | Notes |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Monthly | $199 | $199 | Standard entry |
| Quarterly | $159/mo | $477 billed quarterly | Commitment lever — real conversion target |
| Annual | $129/mo | $1,548 billed annually | Best value — locks cash flow |

Onboarding fee: $149 one-time. This is not a setup charge. It is the Future You formation session — the first time you meet YOU. Positioned and priced as a premium experience, not an administrative cost.

The annual Studio rate at $129/month is structurally significant: it lands at roughly half the cost of assembling a comparable expert tool stack independently. An experienced operator pays $200-275/month to maintain a working stack — and still manages a chaotic subscription log with urgent decisions, overlap, and wasted spend. Studio at $129 annual replaces that entire condition with a curated, Future You-guided operating environment.

## **Pro — The Working Creator**

Future You in peer mode. Open lot. Full platform infrastructure. Tools selected and managed by the creator, accessed at SY enterprise rates. No onboarding fee — Pro creators build their own formation. The tool spread is where SY earns margin on this tier.

| Interval | Price | Effective Monthly | Notes |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Monthly | $149 | $149 | Standard entry |
| Quarterly | $119/mo | $357 billed quarterly |  |
| Annual | $99/mo | $1,188 billed annually |  |

Pro tool spread mechanics: SY negotiates enterprise rates with tool partners. Pro creators access those tools through SY at enterprise rates plus a 15-20% platform margin — still below retail. SY earns the spread. The creator pays less than they would independently and gets the infrastructure layer on top.

## **Founding Member Cohort**

The first 500 members of StudioYou. Annual commitment only — no monthly option. Founding membership is a commitment, not a trial. The onboarding fee is waived for Studio founding members as recognition of early belief. This rate is never available again once the cohort is full.

| Tier | Standard Monthly | Founding Rate | Annual Total | Savings |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Founding Studio | $199/mo | $149/mo | $1,788/yr | $600/yr vs. standard monthly |
| Founding Pro | $149/mo | $99/mo | $1,188/yr | $600/yr vs. standard monthly |

The $149 founding Studio rate carries deliberate psychological weight: one month of founding membership costs exactly what non-founding members pay just to walk in the door. Hard cap at 500 members. The founding cohort shapes the platform culture. There is a real limit to how many people can do that meaningfully.

## **Scholarship Program**

Submission-based. Annual. For creators who can articulate genuine need and what they will build with the platform. Inspired by the a16z community forward model and the Tech Week grant structure — both programs that reward intentionality, not circumstance.

The application itself is the filter. A creator who can write the submission is ready for the platform. The scholarship cohort is expected to produce some of the platform's most compelling early stories. Launched in Phase 2 once the creator base exists to give scholarship membership meaningful context.

# **Ecosystem Revenue**

The creator base is the asset. The ecosystem pays to be near it. No ads. No ownership of creator content. No cut of what creators earn independently. Every ecosystem revenue stream is structured so that SY's commercial interests and the creator's interests point in the same direction.

## **Tool Partner Tiers**

Partners are moons. SY is Jupiter. No partner — at any tier — accesses creator IP, audience data, or relationship records. The carrot is distribution to a pre-qualified, motivated, paying creator base at the exact moment a creator is ready for what they offer. That is not a traffic source. That is a pre-qualified referral with context.

Entry fee structure: one-time, not recurring. When SY says yes to a partner, that is a verdict, not a subscription. The platform has evaluated them, decided they belong on the lot, and made a permanent commitment. Asking for annual re-audition contradicts the authority of that decision. Partners stay because the creator base compounds. The growth is the retention mechanism — not a contract renewal.

| Tier | One-Time Fee | What They Get |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Listed | $2,500 | Directory profile. Passive discovery by creators who seek them. No active reach. |
| Integrated | $15,000 | Native SSO embed, API handoff, featured placement in relevant module, rev share on SaaS subscriptions initiated through SY. |
| Premier | $50,000+ | Category exclusivity option (one-time premium, see below). Co-marketed. Joint roadmap access. Pre-release creator feedback. First look at graduating creators. |

**Revenue Share — Integrated and Premier**

Rev share applies to SaaS and subscription-based tools only. When a Pro creator selects a tool through SY's platform, the partner shares subscription revenue at 15%. No rev share applies to agency, management, or deal-based transactions — that would contradict the constitutional principle.

**Category Exclusivity — Premier Only**

Available as a one-time premium added to the Premier entry fee. Grants exclusivity within a defined tool category for the duration of the partnership. SY retains the unconditional right to add a category or institutional leader — Adobe, Apple, or equivalent — regardless of existing exclusivity grants. This is disclosed to Premier partners at negotiation. The exclusivity is real but not absolute. SY's interests are protected by design.

**Partner Offboarding — The Graduate Pipeline**

When a creator graduates from StudioYou — takes a major deal, joins an agency, scales beyond the platform — they leave with their tool relationships intact. A creator who exits SY and converts directly to a partner's subscription is SY's highest-value delivery to that partner: a power user with documented creative history, established workflow dependency, and zero learning curve. SY takes no fee from that transaction. The partner already paid for access through their entry fee. The graduate is the return on that investment.

## **Brand and Sponsor Access — SPOTStream**

Brands pay to be discoverable by creators. Creators initiate all contact. No cold reach. No unsolicited brand access to creator profiles.

| Revenue Type | Rate | Notes |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Portal access | $1,500/mo | Brand subscription for platform discoverability |
| Performance fee | 12% of deal value | On brand deals closed through SY infrastructure |
| Placement boost | $500-$2,000/campaign | Brand requests priority matching — creator still initiates |

The 12% performance fee is negotiable in early brand conversations. Taking 8% initially to get brands into the ecosystem is a defensible early-stage decision, with the rate rising as the creator base grows. The value of the deal flow compounds with the creator base — the fee structure should reflect that trajectory.

## **Agency and Management Access**

Agencies are tenants, not customers. They pay for access to a pre-qualified, self-selected creator base that SY has already developed. The creator holds all the cards. Agencies are discoverable — they cannot reach creators. Creators find representation when they are ready, on their terms.

The structural protection requires no enforcement. The IP Vault, deal records, and relationship history live inside SY. A creator who takes a deal outside the SY architecture loses the paper trail, the Future You institutional memory, and the protection that made them valuable enough to pursue. Agencies that end-run the platform don't gain access to the infrastructure. They get the talent without the system — a worse deal for them, and they will figure that out.

| Tier | Annual Fee | What They Get |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Listed | $2,500/yr | Directory profile. Discoverable by creators who seek representation. |
| Verified | $7,500/yr | Verified badge, priority placement in creator search, response analytics. |
| Premier | $15,000/yr | Featured placement. Early access to creators exiting the platform — the pre-vetted graduate pipeline no traditional scouting delivers. |

The Premier agency tier is most valuable for one specific reason: first look at SY graduates. A creator who is ready to leave the platform is documented, IP-protected, brand-experienced, and Future You-formed. That pipeline has no equivalent in traditional talent scouting. $15,000/year is not the ceiling for what that access is worth to a boutique management company or agency building a creator roster.

# **Unit Economics — Illustrative**

At 10,000 active Studio subscribers on a blended rate of $160/month (mix of monthly, quarterly, annual):

| Revenue Stream | Monthly | ARR |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Studio subscriptions (10K @ $160 blended) | $1,600,000 | $19.2M |
| Onboarding fees (20% new monthly @ $149) | $298,000 | $3.6M |
| Tool partner ecosystem (50 partners avg $10K) | $42,000 | $500K |
| SPOTStream (200 deals/mo @ $5K avg, 12%) | $120,000 | $1.44M |
| Agency access fees (50 agencies blended) | $25,000 | $300K |
| Total | $2,085,000 | $25M+ |

Creator-side gross margin at this scale: tool and infrastructure costs run approximately $25-30/creator/month, leaving 80-85% gross margin on the subscription revenue. The ecosystem revenue streams carry near-zero marginal cost at scale. The business model is structurally efficient in a way that single-lane tool products are not — because SY earns on the relationship, not the transaction.
