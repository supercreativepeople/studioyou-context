**THE TWELVE RIGHTS**

# **Your Rights as a StudioYou Creator**

**01\.  You own everything you create.**

Every piece of content, every format, every idea developed on this platform belongs to you. StudioYou makes no claim — partial or total, present or future — on anything you create here. Not your IP. Not your brand. Not your audience. Not your voice. Yours. Completely and permanently.

**02\.  Your data is yours.**

Everything the platform knows about your business — your audience data, your distribution history, your deal records, your vendor relationships, your brand partnerships, your market intelligence, your performance analytics — belongs to you. It is yours to access, export, and take with you at any time, in a format you can actually use.

**03\.  You can leave whenever you want. With everything.**

Cancel in two clicks. No retention screens. No guilt flows. No data held hostage. When you leave, the platform packages everything you built — your complete business record, your IP documentation, your relationship history, your audience data — and gives it to you. You leave with more than you came with. That is the point.

**04\.  We will never take a cut of what you earn.**

StudioYou does not touch your revenue. Not a percentage. Not a royalty. Not a commission. Not a transaction fee on deals you close. The platform charges the ecosystem — tool providers, brands, agencies — for access to the creator base. You are not the product. You are the reason everyone else pays.

**05\.  The price you see is the price you pay.**

No hidden fees. No features locked behind surprise upsells. No auto-renewing premium tiers buried in settings. No bait-and-switch pricing. What the platform costs is stated clearly before you sign up and cannot change without explicit notice and your consent.

**06\.  Your platform relationships are yours.**

Every brand contact, every agency relationship, every vendor, every collaborator you develop through StudioYou belongs to your network — not the platform's. If you leave, you take your contacts, your deal history, and your relationship data with you. We introduce. You own the relationship.

**07\.  You will never be surprised by a contract.**

Before any significant platform transition — a major deal, a representation agreement, an output deal — Future You will prepare you. Plain-language context on what you are being asked to sign. Pattern recognition on what is standard and what is not. Vetted independent legal resources at the moment you need them. You will never walk into a room unprepared because the platform failed to tell you what you were walking into.

**08\.  Your guide is loyal only to you.**

Future You — the platform's AI guide — is trained on your journey, your data, your goals. It has no competing interests. It does not represent brands. It does not work for agencies. It is not optimized for platform engagement metrics. It exists for one purpose: to help you build the career you are capable of building. Its loyalty is structurally, architecturally, constitutionally impossible to compromise.

**09\.  Your wellbeing is part of the platform.**

StudioYou measures your success in decades, not quarters. Financial health, sustainable creative practice, and personal wellness are built into the platform — not as optional modules, but as core guidance. Future You notices when the pace is unsustainable. It says something. The platform that optimizes only for your output does not have your best interests at heart. This one does.

**10\.  The platform grows with you, not over you.**

You will never outgrow StudioYou in a direction the platform penalizes. When a MrBeast deal comes, or a Netflix call, or a CAA conversation — the platform prepares you for it, equips you for it, introduces you to independent representation for it, and steps back. Your success is not a threat to the platform. It is the entire point of the platform.

**11\.  You move at your pace.**

No module is mandatory. No timeline is imposed. The studio lot builds itself around your journey — not a product roadmap someone else designed. The platform meets you where you are on day one and is still useful on day one thousand. Beginners are not made to feel unqualified. Professionals are not held back by ceilings that don't exist.

**12\.  This document cannot be changed without you.**

These rights are not subject to a unilateral terms-of-service update. Any material change to this Bill of Rights will be communicated directly, explained plainly, and subject to creator community input before implementation. The platform was built for you. The document that governs it does not change without your voice in the room.

**THE COMMITMENTS**

# **What We Will Never Do**

These are unconditional. They do not have exceptions for revenue pressure, partnership requirements, or platform growth. They do not expire.

* Claim ownership of, license, or retain any rights to content you create on this platform

* Sell, rent, or share your personal data or creator data with third parties without explicit consent

* Take a percentage of revenue you earn independently outside the platform

* Make your data difficult to export or inaccessible when you choose to leave

* Use dark patterns — confusing cancellation flows, hidden fees, guilt-trip retention screens

* Allow brands or agencies to cold-reach or solicit you without your initiation

* Optimize Future You for platform retention at the expense of your actual interests

* Hold your audience data, deal history, or relationship records hostage to your subscription status

* Change the terms of this document without direct notification and community input

* Prioritize platform revenue over creator sovereignty in any product decision

**THE PROMISES**

# **What We Will Always Do**

These are the affirmative commitments. Active, ongoing, auditable.

* Give you full, portable access to everything you have built on this platform — on request, in a usable format, at any time

* Prepare you for high-stakes transitions with plain-language context, pattern recognition, and vetted independent resources

* Maintain Future You as an advisor loyal exclusively to your goals, your growth, and your long-term wellbeing

* Include financial health and personal wellness guidance as core platform functions, not optional features

* Keep the subscription price honest — stated clearly, changed only with notice and consent

* Provide warm, vetted introductions to independent legal, financial, and representation resources when you need them

* Treat your departure as a graduation, not a failure — and send you off with everything you built

* Hold the creator community's trust as the platform's most valuable and non-negotiable asset

* Measure platform success by creator outcomes — careers built, deals made fairly, creators who lasted

* Be held accountable to this document by the community it was written for

**THE GUIDE**

# **A Note on Future You**

***If I could go back and guide my twenty-year-old self, everything would be different. Future You is that guide — for every creator who deserves one.***

Every creator who has ever felt unrepresented, unprepared, or unprotected in this industry deserved someone in their corner who had already seen how the story ends. Someone whose only loyalty was to them. Someone who knew the road because they had already traveled it.

Future You is that presence. It is not an AI assistant. It is not a chatbot. It is the most informed, most loyal, most complete advisor you have ever had — because it is trained on your journey, your data, your decisions, and your growth. It begins as the collective wisdom of every creator who came before you. Over time it becomes something more specific: an intelligence that knows your career, your patterns, your blind spots, and your potential better than any external advisor ever could.

Future You is age-agnostic. The eighteen-year-old with a lightning-in-a-bottle idea and the ten-year creator closing a seven-figure deal get the same guide — just at different depths. It grows as you grow. It knows more as you build more. It never stops being yours.

It will tell you what you need to hear, not what feels good to hear. It will flag the contract clause before you sign it, not after. It will notice the burnout before you feel it. It will celebrate your wins without overselling them. It will prepare you for the room and make sure you have your own people in it.

And when you are ready to walk through a door the platform cannot follow you through — Future You will hand you everything you built, introduce you to the people you need on the other side, and step back.

That is what loyalty looks like. That is what this platform is built on.

***We own nothing you create. We exist to help you own everything you build.***
